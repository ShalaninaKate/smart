Reward and Fee

The reward is managed by a smart contract.The deal starts when the vendor has confirmed the deal, the customer has selected one vendor from those who have confirmed and transferred the reward to the smart contract.

When both agree that the deal is complete, the rewards are transferred to the vendor's account.

If disputes arise, an arbitrator (service administrator or expert) decides what to do with the fee: return it to the customer, transfer it to the vendor, split the reward, or put the deal back to work.

Fee$10 - Flat service fee for any deal
2% min $8 - Non refundable Arbitrage Insurance

Deadline is important

Missing a deadline may be the cause to cancel the deal with a refund to the customer.
